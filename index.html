Programmable is a crypto intelligence company. We build systems that measure, classify and predict the real-world impact of protocols in real time. The best crypto investors use these systems to back impactful innovation, with rewards coming from beating the market.

Permissionless Innovation

We focus on protocols that unlock permissionless innovation. Fundamentally, there are two types of crypto advocate. The first believes that programmable money will eat fiat currency. These are the Bitcoin maximalists. The second believes that real disruption will come from making valuable assets openly programmable. They believe that smart contracts are permissionless APIs, and these APIs will democratise and accelerate innovation. We hold the second position.
