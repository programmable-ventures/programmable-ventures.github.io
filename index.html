Permissionless Innovation

We invest in protocols that enable permissionless innovation. Fundamentally, there are two types of crypto advocate. The first believes that programmable money will eat fiat currency. These are the Bitcoin maximalists. The second believes that real disruption will come from making valuable assets openly programmable. They agree with Vitalik. They believe that smart contracts are permissionless APIs, and these APIs will democratise and accelerate innovation. Ours is the second position.

Permissionless Investment

We follow a permissionless investment process. Unlike traditional venture capital firms, we do not ask that founders share their order books or analytics data. We do not ask for demonstrations, design documents, or code samples. This is because smart-contract transactions are recorded on-chain, and because protocols are open source. The challenge, of course, is to know what to do with all the data and code that is now openly available. This is our unfair advantage. We measure, classify and predict real-world impact in real time, and we buy and sell protocol tokens accordingly.
